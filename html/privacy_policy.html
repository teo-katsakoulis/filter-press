Privacy Policy for FilterPress

FilterPress is a browser extension designed to improve the readability of the comment section using Commento by allowing users to hide comments from specific usernames.

This extension runs entirely on your device. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All settings, including your list of blocked usernames, are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's extension storage.

FilterPress does not connect to any external servers, does not track user behavior, and does not display ads.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to the developer.
